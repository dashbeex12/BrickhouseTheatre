Our History

Brickhouse Theatre Company was born from the ambition and grit of student artists who sought to create bold, original work in unexpected places. Our founding members, driven by the need to reclaim storytelling for themselves and their peers, started with site-specific pieces performed in outdoor courtyards, empty classrooms, and community spaces.

Our Philosophy

We believe theatre should be daring, immersive, and deeply connected to its community. We center stories from unheard voices, emphasize collaboration over hierarchy, and support a wide array of talents both onstage and off. Our productions are fueled by creativity, empathy, and a spirit of radical experimentation.

Our Vision

As we grow, Brickhouse Theatre hopes to become a launchpad for emerging creatives across disciplines.
